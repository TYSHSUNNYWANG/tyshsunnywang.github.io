Biggest change in my life


-Martial Arts team

When I went to third grade, I joined the Martial arts team. I initiallu treated this team as a entertaining club. But after a year's training. It totally changed my mindset.

My coach is literally my second dad. Due the fact that my mom is usually working until midnight, so I had to have my dinner by myself. But as i join martial arts team, my coach always ask me to join their family to have dinner after training, then he just brought me to home. I was so thankful that he let me realize how is it feel like to have dinner with family.

Not only improve my health condition, the most important of all is that it teaches my how to control my temper properly and to forgive others.
